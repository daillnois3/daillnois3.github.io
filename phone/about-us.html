Bishop Frederick Barr & Dr. Erica Barr

 

This unique husband and wife ministry team has inspired people everywhere to accept nothing but God's best! Together they motivate others to establish and preserve strong family bonds, marriages, relationships and become all that God has created them to be.

Bishop Frederick Barr

Bishop Frederick Barr D.D is the founder and senior pastor of LIFE Church International in Nashville, Tennessee. He has been the loving husband of Co-Pastor Dr. Erica Barr for twenty years.

He has traveled the world extensively evangelizing and preaching the good news of Jesus Christ as an international recording artist, and as a facilitator of marriage and relationship conferences for the United States Armed Forces.  Dr. Frederick Barr answered the call to preach the Gospel at the age of twelve, and faithfully served as a religious leader for over  thirty years.

 He recently retired from a successful career with the Saturn Corporation.

 

Dr. Barr currently serves as the Pastoral Care & Community Outreach Coordinator, Public Relations Director, and Bishop of the LIFE Church’s International.

 

Dr. Frederick Barr pursued his education in Business and Religious studies at Oakland University, Middle Tennessee University, David Lipscomb University and received a Doctorate in Theology from Emmanuel Baptist University. Among his many accomplishments, Dr. Barr was bestowed the honor of Colonel of the state of Kentucky; making him the goodwill ambassador for the region.

Dr. Erica Barr

Dr. Erica Barr Co-pastors alongside her husband, Bishop Frederick Barr, at LIFE Church International located in  Nashville, Tennessee.

,She is an international recording artist, talk show host, relationship and life coach, and facilitator of marriage and relationship conferences, seminars and workshops presented around the world.

 

Dr. Erica Barr has maintained a successful career in television and radio broadcasting and received her educational studies at Tennessee State University, Middle Tennessee State University, and Nashville State College, obtaining her degree in Business Administration and a Doctorate in Theology from Emmanuel Baptist University. She currently serves as the Executive Administrator, Director of Women’s Department and Ministerial Training at Life Church International. Dr. Erica Barr has authored manuscripts involving ministry, healing, marriage and relationships. Recently she has written a book titled “Relationship Monsters”. Dr. Barr has been happily married for twenty years to Bishop Frederick Barr. Through teaching, preaching, and the prophetic ministry, she is equipping God’s people to become People of Hope, People of Purpose, and People of Destiny!
